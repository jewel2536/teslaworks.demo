What is Tesla Works?

Tesla Works is a project-based community of makers that brings together passionate and goal-oriented students to turn ideas into reality. By providing funding, work space, and an environment for collaboration, Tesla Works fosters unbridled creativity and builds skills to solve 21st century problems. Read more about Tesla Works

Getting Started

Do you have a project idea you want to get started on? We can't wait to help you begin! There are a few steps you'll need to take before you can begin turning your project into reality. Get started now!

Projects

Tesla Works provides university students the opportunity to design and build innovative projects across a variety of disciplines. Our unique community brings together students from a variety of majors to collaborate with one another and create novel, innovative machines, artwork, programs, and demonstrations.
